Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2, 2026

Welcome to DeenNotes. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Summary: DeenNotes is designed with privacy in mind. Your journal entries and prayer data are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your personal reflections or prayer history.

1. Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Journal Entries: Your personal reflections, thoughts, and journal content
  • Prayer Tracking Data: Records of your prayer completion
  • App Preferences: Your settings, themes, and notification preferences
  • Prayer Time Settings: Your preferred calculation method and madhab

Information Used for App Functionality

  • Location Data: With your permission, we access your device's location to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction. This data is processed locally on your device and is not stored on external servers.
  • Device Information: Basic device information may be collected for app functionality and crash reporting.

2. Third-Party Services

AI Guidance Feature

DeenNotes offers an optional AI guidance feature that provides Islamic spiritual insights. When you use this feature:

  • Your questions or prompts are sent to OpenAI's API for processing
  • We do not store your AI conversations on our servers
  • OpenAI processes this data according to their Privacy Policy
  • We recommend not sharing personally identifiable information in AI conversations

Apple App Store

If you make in-app purchases (premium subscription), the transaction is processed by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy practices are governed by their Privacy Policy.

Google Play Store

For Android users, in-app purchases are processed by Google Play. We do not have access to your payment information. Google's privacy practices are governed by their Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide accurate prayer times based on your location
  • Display the correct Qibla direction
  • Send prayer time notifications (if enabled)
  • Provide AI-powered spiritual guidance (when requested)
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs

4. Data Storage and Security

Your personal data, including journal entries and prayer records, is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by the operating system. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information.

Important: Since your data is stored locally, if you uninstall the app or reset your device, your journal entries and prayer history will be permanently deleted. We recommend regularly backing up your device.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law or to respond to legal process
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • With service providers who assist in app operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your data:

  • Location Access: You can enable or disable location access in your device settings at any time
  • Notifications: You can manage notification preferences within the app or through device settings
  • Data Deletion: You can delete your journal entries and prayer history within the app, or by uninstalling the app
  • AI Feature: Use of the AI guidance feature is optional

7. Children's Privacy

DeenNotes is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
